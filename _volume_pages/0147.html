THE OL' TUNES.
YOU kin talk about yer anthems
An' arias
yer an' sich,
An' modern
yer choir-singin'
That think
you awful
so rich ;
But you orter heerd us youngsters
In the times far
now away,
A-singin' o' the ol' tunes
In the ol'-fashioned way.
There was some of us sung treble
An' few
a of us growled bass,
An' the tide o' flowed
song smoothly
With its 'comp'niment o' grace ;
There spirit
was in that music,
An' kind
a o' solemn sway,
A-singin' o' the ol' tunes
In the ol'-fashioned way.
