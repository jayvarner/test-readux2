HYMN.
WHEN storms arise
And dark'ning skies
About threat'ning lower,
me
To thee, O Lord, I raise mine eyes,
To thee tortured spirit flies
my
For solace in that hour.
Thy mighty arm
Will let harm
no
Come befall
near me nor me ;
Thy voice shall quiet alarm,
my
When life's great battle waxeth warm ■
No foeman shall appall me.
Upon thy breast
Secure I rest,
From and vexation
sorrow ;
oppressed,
No by sinful
more cares
But in thy blest,
presence ever
O God of salvation.
my
