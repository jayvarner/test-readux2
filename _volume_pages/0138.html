BY THE STREAM.
BY the stream I dream in calm delight, and
watch in
as glass,
a
How the clouds like crowds of snowy-hued and
white-robed maidens pass,
And the water into ripples breaks and sparkles
as it spreads,
Like host of
a armored knights with silver
helmets their heads.
on
And I deem the stream emblem
an fit of human
life may go,
For I find mind
a may sparkle much and yet
but shallows show,
And soul
a may glow with myriad lights and
wondrous mysteries,
When it only lies dormant
a thing and mirrors
what it sees.
