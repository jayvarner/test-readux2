BEYOND THE YEARS.
i.
BEYOND the the lies,
years answer
Beyond where brood the grieving skies
And Night drops tears.
Where Faith rod-chastened smiles to rise
And doff its fears,
And carping Sorrow pines and dies —
Beyond the years.
ii.
Beyond the the for
years prayer rest
Shall beat within the breast
no more ;
The darkness clears,
And Morn perched the mountain's
on crest
Her form uprears —
The day that is to is best,
come
Beyond the years.
